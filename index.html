About Me

I'm Tebogo Mathaba, a final-year software development student at WeThinkCode, with a background in Information Technology and Computer Science (2018–2020) and a certificate in Information and Management Systems from Alison. My professional journey includes roles as a Scanner/Counter at Ghekko, Management Assistant at Volkswagen, Assistant Administrator at Founder Institute, and Assistant Manager at Investable, alongside training with Huawei (2020) and Mercer (2021).

I specialize in creating data-driven solutions, such as management dashboards in Power BI, and have developed applications using Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, C#, and SQL. My certifications include Microsoft Azure Developer Associate (AZ-204), Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900), Microsoft Digital Literacy, Web Designer Certification, and Project Planning (Spec Con).

Beyond technical skills, I bring strong leadership and communication abilities, honed as Editor-in-Chief of my school newspaper and through volunteer work with Higher Health and NOSA (First Aid Level 1). I'm excited to combine my passion for technology and business management to tackle new challenges.
